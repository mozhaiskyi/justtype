# JustType — milestone popup strategy

## When to show

A one-time popup triggered at usage milestones.

| Milestone | Trigger |
|---|---|
| 100 uses | Popup |
| 1,000 uses | Popup |

---

## Copy

**Headline**

> You've just typed [N] times. That's the whole idea.

**Supporting line**

> Made by one person, free forever. If it's saving you time, even a small tip means a lot.

**Buttons**

| Action | Label |
|---|---|
| Primary CTA | Buy me a coffee |
| Dismiss | Maybe later |

---

## Rules

- No countdown, no guilt copy, no "keep JustType free"
- Link primary CTA to Buy Me a Coffee or Ko-fi
